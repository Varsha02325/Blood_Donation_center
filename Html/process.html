Blood Donation Process

1. Registration

  • You provide identification and basic details like name, age, and medical history.
  • Some centers may require you to complete a short questionnaire about your health and lifestyle.

2. Health Screening

  • A healthcare professional checks your hemoglobin levels (via a finger prick test).
  • Your blood pressure, pulse, and temperature are measured.
  • You may be asked about recent travel, medications, or medical conditions.

3.Donation Process

  • You will be seated or asked to lie down.
  • A sterile needle is inserted into a vein in your arm, and blood is collected into a bag.
  • The donation process usually takes 8-10 minutes, and around 350-450 ml of blood is collected.

4.Recovery & Refreshments

  • After donation, you will rest for a few minutes and receive fluids or light snacks to help recover.
